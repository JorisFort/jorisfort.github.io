Bot Boxer

The bot boxer is a new and innovative way to train and become better. It is an interactive experience. Where you could play against virtual opponents if you have the play variant of the bot boxer. The robot learns your movement and predicts where you will hit and evade your move. On the included screen you can see a lot of statistics and the difficulty of the robot. You can change the difficulty of the bot so that it makes more mistakes or that the robot will react slower to your punches.

Ski and Snowboard Simulator

Just like the bot boxer to simulate boxing, there is also a simulator for skiing and snowboarding. This simulator is on a big motorized machine where you can move from left to right to move on the virtual ski slope. With a big panoramic screen in front of you to completely immerse yourself. Everything gets tracked from the calories you burn to how many turns you made on the virtual slope. There is also a leaderboard where you can compete with other people.

Apple Watch Ultra

The new apple watch ultra comes with a lot of new sport-related features. For example, the watch is designed to comply with EN13319. Which is a diving accessories standard for how deep a device works underwater. But the apple watch ultra is also great for traveling and mountain climbing with the new siren which you can use in case of an emergency. Or for running with the new running app that tracks how far your steps are and where you can race against your past self.
